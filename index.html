Frequently asked questions

What is Axolotlies? +
Axolotlies is a collection of 999 hand-drawn, impossibly cute axolotls living on Ethereum. They're free to mint and built for a warm, friendly community.
How much does it cost? +
It's a free mint — you only cover the standard Ethereum network gas fee when you claim. No hidden price.
How do I get on the allowlist? +
Join the waitlist and finish the four quick steps on the application page. Completed entries are reviewed for the allowlist.
When is mint day? +
The exact date is announced on our X account. Follow @Axolotlies_NFT so you're the first to know.
Which wallet do I need? +
Any Ethereum (EVM) wallet works — MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet and similar. Just paste your public address; never share your seed phrase with anyone.
